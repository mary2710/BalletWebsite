My Favourite Dancers

There are many famous ballet dancers around the world, but here are some of my favourites:


Marianela Nunez

  • Age: 37 (23rd of March 1982)
  • Birthplace: San Martin, Argentina
    • Marianela Nunez is currently a Principal Dancer with the Royal Ballet in London.

One of my favourite performances was of Marianela dancing in the Black Swan coda scene in Swan Lake act 3.
<


Natalia Osipova

  • Age: 33 (18th of May 1986)
  • Birthplace: Moscow, Russia
    • Natalia Osipova is currently a Principal Dancer with The Royal Ballet in London.


Akane Takada

  • Age: 29 (18th April 1990)
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
    • Akane Takada is currently a Principal Dancer with the Royal Ballet in London.


Kathryn Morgan

  • Age: 30 (17th of August 1989)
  • Birthplace: North Carolina, USA
    • Kathryn Morgan is currently a Soloist at Miami City Ballet, and was formerly a Soloist at New York City Ballet.


Miko Fogarty

  • Age: 22 (23rd of May 1997)
  • Birthplace: London, England
    • As a child, Miko Fogarty won many international ballet competitions, and featured in the TV Series 'First Position', which made her very well known among the ballet community. Although, recently, Miko has decided to take a break from Ballet and is currently studying biology at The University of California.
